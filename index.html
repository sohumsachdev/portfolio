  • GPA Calculators

    I created a set of three GPA calculators for different grading systems, but the most popular one is the Severn Upper School GPA Calculator. I originally made it just to help myself understand how my classes would affect my GPA, but once I shared it, it took off fast. According to Google Analytics, the calculators have 738 active users. What means the most to me is how many students have told me in person that it helped them. Seeing that something I built help people has been my favorite part. This project taught me a lot about frontend development, interpreting real-world rules into code, and building tools that solve actual problems for real people.
